Autism Resources for California Families

If you’re exploring an autism diagnosis, school supports, or services in California, you’re not alone. We’ve gathered trusted starting points below to help you find information and next steps.

Need immediate help? Call 911 for emergencies. For crisis support, call or text 988.

How to Use This Page

  • Start Here: Learn where to begin after a diagnosis or concern.
  • School Supports: Find California school resources for IEP and 504 planning.
  • Services & Programs: Locate DDS and Regional Center support options.
  • Teens & Adults: Explore transition planning resources for later stages.
